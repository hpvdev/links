Privacy Policy for Japly QR Code & Barcode Scanner App

We welcome you to use the Japly QR Code & Barcode Scanner app ("we," "app," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using this app, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

Information Collection:

In addition to camera access for direct QR code scanning, we request the following permissions:

  • Read Contacts (android.permission.READ_CONTACTS): We request this permission to enable the app to add contact information to your device's address book after scanning QR codes that contain contact information.
  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): This permission is used for scanning QR codes directly through your device's camera.
  • Record Audio (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO): This permission may be used for future features that require audio input. Currently, this feature is under development, and the app does not store or transmit audio data.

We do not access, store, or share any personal information from your contacts, camera, or microphone without your consent.

Use of Information:

Information from your device's camera and contacts is used solely for QR code scanning and adding contact information to your address book, respectively. We do not store or use this information for any other purposes.

Information Security:

We implement appropriate security measures to ensure that information from your device is safely protected.

Information Sharing:

We do not share information from your device with third parties without your consent.

Policy Changes:

We may update this privacy policy over time. Any changes will be posted on our website.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us via email at scanandgenerate@gmail.com.
