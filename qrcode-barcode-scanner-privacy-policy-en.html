Information from images in your photo library or camera is used solely for QR code scanning and providing corresponding results to you. We do not store or use this information for any other purposes.

Information Security:

We implement appropriate security measures to ensure that information from images in your photo library or camera is safely protected.

Information Sharing:

We do not share information from images in your photo library or camera with third parties.
